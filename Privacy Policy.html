1. Introduction

At Etech Digital Solution, your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you interact with our website, products, and services. By using our platforms, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, billing details, business name.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device info, pages visited, time on site.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share your data only:

All your information is stored securely using encrypted servers and access controls. We implement modern cybersecurity measures including firewalls, 2FA, and regular vulnerability assessments.
