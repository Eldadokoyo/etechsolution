Starlink (Chemist Wi-Fi) – Internet Access System

Installed and configured a Starlink internet system at a chemist. Set up a manual client notification system to inform users of their session time, helping manage customer traffic without extra software costs.

AfyaLink Health Systems

Developed a secure and user-friendly health records system used by over 50 clinics across Kenya. The platform allowed CHPs and medical officers to track patient data, schedule follow-ups, and generate reports—all while meeting data privacy regulations.

EdTech 360

Designed and launched an intuitive learning management system used by schools and independent tutors. Features included interactive quizzes, student progress tracking, and mobile-first video lessons optimized for low-bandwidth areas.

RuralNet

Led the setup of solar-powered Wi-Fi zones in underserved rural areas using mesh networking and Starlink backhaul. Deployed user login and M-PESA-based access management systems, giving over 5,000 users affordable internet access.

Global Gospel Network

Created a cross-platform app for churches worldwide to livestream services, host Bible study content, and receive donations. The platform supports multi-language content, user subscriptions, and community chat features.

Safari Luxe Villas

Developed a luxury brand for Safari Luxe Villas in Naivasha, including a minimal logo, elegant color scheme, and a responsive booking website. Integrated calendar sync, WhatsApp chat, and secure online payments to enhance guest experience.

AfriTech Summit 2021

Worked with the AfriTech Summit team to create a futuristic brand look for their 2029 conference. Delivered a sleek landing page with session schedules, speaker profiles, and registration forms. Also provided matching social media kits and digital banners for promotion.

PawPal Kenya

Created a warm and playful brand for a startup offering pet services in Nairobi. Designed a logo, social media templates, and a WordPress site featuring a booking form, blog section, and service listing. The site helped them reach over 10,000 pet owners in the first year.
